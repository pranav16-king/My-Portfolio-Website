# 📖 Overview

This repository documents our team's participation in **IDEATHON 9.0**, a **National Level Innovation Competition** focused on transforming creative ideas into impactful solutions for real-world challenges.

IDEATHON 9.0 provided an excellent platform for students, innovators, entrepreneurs, and researchers to present innovative concepts across multiple technology domains, receive expert feedback, and compete with teams from institutions across India.

The competition encouraged participants to identify pressing societal and industrial problems, develop scalable technology-driven solutions, and present their ideas through a professional pitch deck before an expert evaluation panel.
