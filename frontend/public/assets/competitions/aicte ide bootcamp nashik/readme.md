# 📖 Overview

This repository documents our participation in the **Innovation, Design and Entrepreneurship (IDE) Bootcamp – Edition 3 (Phase I)**, a national innovation and entrepreneurship development program organized under the **All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)** in collaboration with the **Wadhwani Foundation** and supported by the **Ministry of Education, Government of India**.

The bootcamp was designed to nurture innovation, entrepreneurial thinking, design methodologies, and startup development among engineering students. Participants engaged in hands-on learning, mentorship sessions, idea validation workshops, and business model development activities.

# 🎯 Program Objectives

The IDE Bootcamp focused on developing the entrepreneurial mindset and innovation capabilities of students through practical learning and mentorship.

The primary objectives included:

* Promoting innovation-driven problem solving
* Encouraging entrepreneurship among students
* Understanding Design Thinking methodology
* Learning startup ideation and validation
* Developing sustainable business models
* Building investor-ready innovation concepts
* Enhancing leadership and teamwork skills
* Transforming ideas into scalable ventures

# 🚀 Program Workflow

The bootcamp followed a structured innovation journey:

1. Problem Identification
2. Design Thinking Workshop
3. User Research
4. Idea Generation
5. Solution Validation
6. Prototype Planning
7. Business Model Development
8. Pitch Preparation
9. Mentor Feedback
10. Final Presentation
